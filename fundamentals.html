Hardware

Hardware includes the physical parts of a computer system, such as the motherboard, processor, memory, storage devices, and peripherals.

Software

Software includes operating systems, applications, and utilities that allow users and systems to perform tasks.

Networking

Networking connects devices so they can communicate and share resources. Important concepts include IP addresses, routers, switches, and internet connectivity.
