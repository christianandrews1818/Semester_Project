Threats

Threats are potential dangers to systems and data, including malware, phishing attacks, ransomware, and unauthorized access.

Vulnerabilities

Vulnerabilities are weaknesses in software, hardware, or processes that attackers can exploit.

Protection Methods

Basic protection methods include strong passwords, multi-factor authentication, software updates, firewalls, antivirus tools, and security awareness training.
